# Customer Success Health Score Dashboard

An interactive dashboard that calculates a customer health score using sample Customer Success metrics.

## Why I built this
I created this project to demonstrate how Customer Success teams can use data to assess account health, identify potential risk, and prioritize proactive outreach.

## What it does
Users can select a company from a dropdown and view:
- SLA Score
- Incident Score
- CSAT Score
- Adoption Score
- Overall Health Score
- Status label: Healthy, Watch, or At Risk

## Scoring model
The overall health score is calculated as the average of:
- SLA
- Incident
- CSAT
- Adoption

### Status thresholds
- 90–100 = Healthy
- 75–89 = Watch
- Below 75 = At Risk

## Files
- `index.html` = page structure
- `style.css` = styling
- `script.js` = dashboard logic
- `README.md` = project summary

## Why this matters for Customer Success
A health score model helps Customer Success teams:
- identify churn risk earlier
- monitor service quality and customer sentiment
- prioritize strategic outreach
- communicate account health clearly
